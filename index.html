Why Paisan

Food costs are rising. We keep them low — together.

Independent Italian restaurants pay retail prices on nearly everything that comes through the back door. Paisan pools the buying power of operators across the country so every member gets chain-level pricing on the ingredients they already use.

The Paisan promise

“We charge nothing, you change nothing.”

It's free to join and free to be a member. You don't need to change your preferred distributor, your menu, or the way you run your kitchen. You keep doing what you do — we go to work behind the scenes negotiating better prices and rebates on your behalf.
