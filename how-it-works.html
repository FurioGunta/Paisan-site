How it works

Simple for the restaurant. Powerful for the group.

Four steps. No fees. No distributor change. You keep doing what you do — Paisan handles the rest.

  1. 01

    Join free

    Sign up in minutes. No cost, no monthly fee, no long contract. Keep your distributor, your menu, and your team exactly as they are.

  2. 02

    Paisan negotiates

    We go to work behind the scenes — securing manufacturer rebates, contract pricing, and distributor programs on the Italian ingredients you already buy. Cheese, tomatoes, olive oil, flour, meats, paper goods.

  3. 03

    You earn and save

    Lower invoice prices from Day 1, plus quarterly rebate checks for purchases you were already making. Real money back in your pocket, every quarter, with no change to how you order.

  4. 04

    Mangia together

    The more restaurants in the group, the more we all save. Paisan is a community of Italian operators looking out for each other — sharing what works, introducing new members, and keeping our kitchens strong for the next generation.
